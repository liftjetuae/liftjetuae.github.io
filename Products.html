PALLET TRUCK

2000kg Nylon Wheel Pallet Truck with Single Load Roaders, a budget pallet truck solution.

This model has Nylon single load rollers and has a 2000kg capacity. The pallet truck chock is made from a hard rigid moulded plastic construction and are a durable product with an extensive product lifecycle even when used regularly.

This product chocks the steer wheel(s) in place, meaning the wheels will stay fixed in one place. The bottom section consists of a section of suction cups grips for added bonding to the floor surface.

A pallet truck chock, which is also known as a pallet truck stop, is a pu wedge wich has a v-slot in it, this slot immobilises the pallet trucks rear wheels, whilst the weight of the pallet truck itself holds the chock securely in position. A pallet truck stop could mean no more loose pallet trucks causing damage to vehicles or freight whilst in transit. One addition benefit of a pallet truck stop, is that drivers no longer have to risk injury by laying the pallet truck on it’s side, or lifting the truck into a tyre.

The Pallet Truck Chock: Dimensions: 250mm long x 320mm wide.
