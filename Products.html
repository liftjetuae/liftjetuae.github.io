Steel Wire Ropes

Kiswire Ltd. is the largest steel rope manufacturer in the world. The manufacturing giant produces an impressive range of steel wire rope products of various constructions, lays, cores & specialised applications. Established in 1945, Kiswire has now reached an output capacity of 1.0 million metric tons per year of high carbon steel wire products alone. Amzone partnered up with Kiswire Ltd as its exclusive sales & marketing office in the region and declares their 20 year association with each other. Any Kiswire Steel Rope Dia between 3 mm and 180 mm can be found in our portfolio of products.

What is Alumar? "Alumar" is a unique technology that improves corrosion resistance of steel wire ropes by 50% using a zinc and 5% aluminium coating.

Make a special request to your Amzone Sales Manager for your steel wire ropes to be Alumar coated!

Synthetic Ropes

Dong Yang was founded in 1949 as a Fiber Rope Specialist. The company developed a new special yarn called the “MirAcle Yarn” made from Ultra High Molecular Weight Polyethylene or UHMWPE. The MirAcle Yarn is the strongest fibre in the world and is approximately 15 times stronger than steel. Dong Yang uses this yarn to produce their new age synthetic ropes including TERAMAX, TERAMAX-PLUS, MEGACORE, MEGALON and TERAFLEX.

Amzone International joined forces with Dong Yang to distribute their high-quality, high-performance products that have been now servicing the industry for decades. Dong Yang’s experience and world class quality of products comply with requirements of ISO 9001, KS, JIS, Lloyds, ABS, DNV, NK, CCS and BV certificates. All mooring tails are fabricated and then delivered by Amzone International under the Dong Yang quality of excellence.
